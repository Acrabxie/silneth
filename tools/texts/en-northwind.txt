The North Wind and the Sun disputed which was the stronger.
A traveler came along wrapped in a warm cloak.
They agreed that the one who first made the traveler take off his cloak was the stronger.
The North Wind blew as hard as he could.
But the more he blew, the more closely the traveler wrapped his cloak around him.
At last the North Wind gave up.
Then the Sun shone warmly.
The traveler felt the warm rays.
He took off his cloak.
So the North Wind confessed that the Sun was the stronger of the two.
