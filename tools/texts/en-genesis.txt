In the beginning the gods made the sky and the earth.
The earth was empty, and darkness lay upon the deep water.
And the gods said, let there be light. And there was light.
The gods saw the light, and it was good.
They divided the light from the darkness.
They called the light day, and the darkness they called night.
The gods made two great lights.
The greater light ruled the day, and the lesser light ruled the night.
They made the stars also.
